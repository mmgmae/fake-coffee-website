Our Roastory

Brutal Koffe Co.

BRUTAL KOFFE CO. STARTED in 20XX with a mission to roast the most brutal and sick coffee known to man. Though fictional, that doesn't stop our roasters from killin' it. We choose the most unique coffee and roast with precision to bring the best that bean can offer to your cup.

At our roastery, we focus on quality and sustainability. We source our beans from farmers whose working conditions are anything but brutal. We source beans from single-origin farmers who take pride in the quality of their product. We ensure that the farms we work with have safe conditions and happy farmers. We source single origin so we can control our farmer-distributor-roaster relationship.
